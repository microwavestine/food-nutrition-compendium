# Avocado Pasta
RECIPE (4 servings each 430cal/13P/53C/16F):
-2 avocados
-a big handful basil
-3 big Tbsp vegan creme fraiche/cream cheese
-juice of 1 lemon
-2 Tbsp olive oil
-1 Tsp garlic powder, or grinded garlic
-salt and pepper to taste
-1/2 cup (125ml) pasta water
Mix/grind them well!
-pasta to serve
